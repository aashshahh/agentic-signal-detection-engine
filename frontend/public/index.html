How It Works

The Bayesian model maintains a Beta(α, β) posterior over P(market shift | signal fired).

• When a signal fires AND the market moves → α += 1 (confirmed hit)

• When a signal fires AND the market doesn't move → β += 1 (false alarm)

• Alert fires when posterior mean ≥ 70%

Prior: Beta(2,2) — starts at 50% with mild uncertainty. Converges over time.

Feature Descriptions · per Proposal §6
§6.1 Embedding Drift
Cosine distance between first/second-half SBERT embeddings. High drift = narrative shift in progress.
§6.2 Topic Entropy
Shannon entropy over K-Means topic clusters. Low entropy = converging narrative = signal.
§6.3 Rare Phrase Emergence
N-grams that were historically rare and are now surging. Catches insider-adjacent terminology early.
§6.4 Engagement Burst
Z-score over rolling hourly post volume. Sometimes it's not what is said but how fast it spreads.
